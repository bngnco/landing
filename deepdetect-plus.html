DeepDetect+

Advanced

Advanced deepfake detection

DeepDetect+ is Verifyco's advanced detection engine, combining multi-layer forensic inspection with a neural architecture trained on adversarial deepfakes, generative-AI artifacts, and historic manipulation patterns — designed to surface the evidence that matters.

Powered by on-device AI
CoreML · On-device · Private
HOW IT WORKS

Detection layers.

Six independent forensic methods run on every file, then fuse into a single, explainable verdict.

Multi-layer analysis

Runs five independent detection layers — C2PA, metadata, AI detection, temporal, and frequency analysis.

AI detection

On-device neural network analyzes facial regions, skin texture, and boundary artifacts to identify AI-generated imagery.

Frequency analysis

Spectral analysis detects patterns invisible to the human eye that distinguish synthetic content from authentic media.

Metadata forensics

Examines EXIF, TIFF, IPTC, and PNG metadata for inconsistencies, AI generator signatures, and tampering.

Content provenance

Verifies C2PA content credential signatures embedded by cameras and platforms that support them.

Detailed reports

Export analysis results as PDF or JSON with per-layer breakdowns, confidence levels, and verdict classification.
