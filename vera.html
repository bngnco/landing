03 · ARCHITECTURE

How Vera thinks.

Forensic signals become structured evidence; a reasoning core weighs their agreements and contradictions; the output is a report you can interrogate.

1

STAGE 01Perception

Six independent evidence layers examine the file itself — nothing is taken on trust.

STAGE 02Evidence graph

A multimodal model fine-tuned for forensic reasoning weighs agreements and contradictions across modalities — does the voice match the lips? Does the light match the scene?

04 · PRINCIPLES

Built the way evidence deserves.

Evidence over verdicts

Every claim traceable to a layer, a region, a timestamp. Unavailable signals are declared, never hidden.

Honest uncertainty

"Inconclusive" is a valid answer, and Vera will say it. Confidence you can't justify is confidence you shouldn't ship.

Privacy by architecture

Hybrid design: on-device layers (Neural Engine, DeepDetect+) wherever possible, ephemeral processing elsewhere — and never trained on user media.
