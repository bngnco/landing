01 · The gap

A score is a starting point.

A single probability can tell you something deserves attention. It can't tell you which signals mattered, what conflicts with them, or what to examine next.

27/100 Detector output
  • What caused the score?
  • Which evidence is strongest?

04 · Architecture

How Vera thinks.

Forensic signals become structured evidence; a reasoning core weighs their agreements and contradictions; the output is a report you can interrogate — never a hidden monologue.

1

STAGE 01 · in: mediaReception

Independent evidence layers examine the file itself — nothing is taken on trust.

C2PA Metadata DeepDetect+ neural Temporal Frequency Audio
2

STAGE 02 · out: evidence graphEvidence graph

Raw findings become structured, cross-referenced evidence — each claim tied to the layer, region and timestamp that produced it.

3

STAGE 03 · reasoningReasoning loop

A model designed for forensic reasoning weighs agreements and contradictions across modalities — does the voice match the lips? Does the light match the scene?

4

STAGE 04 · out: reportExamination report

A conversational report: what was found, where, why it matters — and what remains genuinely uncertain.

Vera surfaces evidence summaries and rationale — not hidden internal tokens. Grounded in the research frontier of explainable multimodal forensics · FakeShield, ICLR 2025 →

05 · The wedge

Detection flags. Vera examines.

Vera is a reasoning layer on top of Verifyco's detection — it complements DeepDetect+, it doesn't replace the underlying analysis.

Detection layer
  • Produces signals and scores
  • Flags media for attention
  • Answers the first question
  • Measures confidence
Vera reasoning layer
  • Organises signals into evidence
  • Helps explain why it was flagged
  • Supports follow-up questions
  • Communicates uncertainty and limits
Verifyco detection layers → Vera reasoning layer → iOS · Web · API (planned)
06 · The charter

Built the way evidence deserves.

01

Evidence over verdicts

Every conclusion stays connected to the signals that support — or limit — it. Unavailable signals are declared, never hidden.

02

Uncertainty stays visible

Vera says what it cannot determine, rather than hiding ambiguity behind a confident sentence. "Inconclusive" is a valid answer.

03

Privacy is architectural

Verifyco's detection runs on-device today. Vera's hybrid design keeps as much as possible local and is never trained on your media. Read the privacy policy →

04

Human judgment stays in the loop

Vera supports an investigation; it doesn't make consequential decisions for people. An examination aid, not an oracle.
